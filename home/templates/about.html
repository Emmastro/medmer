About us

Medmer is focused on getting you emergency response whenever and wherever you are It is very simple and easy to use, offering you a much needed help in times you need.

Steps for getting help

Press the Help Button

The red button on the home page is your emergency response button, in light of any medical situation use it.

Indicate your Details

A form will appear and you will to indicate where you current location is and the kind of medical emergency that you need, afterwards click on the submit button.

Stay Put

A medic will be assigned to you based on your current location, and therefore ensure that you do not change location. If you are with someone who is your emergency contact we highly suggest that they offer the support needed before the medic arrives.

Receive the treatement

Once the medic arrives you will receive the first aid treatement you deserve m and if need be, you shall be transported to the nearest health facility.
